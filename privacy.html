Privacy Policy

Overview

ImmiLane ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website immilane.com. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you visit our site.

Information We Collect

We do not require account creation or login. We do not collect personally identifiable information unless you voluntarily provide it through our feedback form (optional email address and message).

Analytics & Cookies

We use the following third-party services that may place cookies or collect anonymized usage data:

  • Google Analytics 4 (GA4) — Collects anonymized data about page views, scroll depth, time on page, and feature usage to help us improve the site. Google's privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy
  • Cloudflare Web Analytics — Privacy-focused analytics that do not use client-side state (cookies) to collect metrics. Cloudflare's privacy policy: cloudflare.com/privacypolicy

Advertising

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google AdSense may use cookies and web beacons to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings or aboutads.info/choices.

Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on your visit to this site and/or other sites on the Internet. You may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google advertising privacy page.

Feedback Form

Our optional feedback form collects:

  • Feedback category and message (required)
  • Email address (optional, only if you want us to follow up)

This data is stored securely and used solely to improve ImmiLane. We do not sell, share, or distribute your feedback data to third parties.

Data Sources

All PERM case data displayed on ImmiLane is sourced from publicly available records at FLAG.DOL.GOV. We do not collect or store individual immigration case details beyond what is publicly available.

Data Retention

Analytics data is retained according to the respective third-party service policies. Feedback submissions are retained indefinitely to help us improve the site, unless you request deletion.

Your Rights

You may:

  • Disable cookies in your browser settings
  • Opt out of Google personalized ads via Google Ads Settings
  • Request deletion of any feedback data you've submitted by contacting us

Children's Privacy

ImmiLane is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you can reach us through the feedback form on our site.
